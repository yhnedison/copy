<p><b>Example:</b></p>
<pre><b>Input:</b> <code>[0,1,0,3,12]</code>
<b>Output:</b> <code>[1,3,12,0,0]</code></pre>
<p><b>Note</b>:</p>
<ol>
<li>You must do this <b>in-place</b> without making a copy of the array.</li>
<li>Minimize the total number of operations.</li>
