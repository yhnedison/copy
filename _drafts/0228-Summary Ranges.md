#### Tags: 'Array'

<pre><b>Input:</b>  [0,1,2,4,5,7]
<b>Output:</b> ["0-&gt;2","4-&gt;5","7"]
<strong>Explanation: </strong>0,1,2 form a continuous range; 4,5 form a continuous range.
</pre>

<b>Output:</b> ["0","2-&gt;4","6","8-&gt;9"]

</pre>
