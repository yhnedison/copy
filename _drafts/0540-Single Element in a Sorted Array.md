#### Tags:

<p> </p>
<p><b>Example 1:</b></p>
<pre><b>Input:</b> [1,1,2,3,3,4,4,8,8]
<b>Output:</b> 2
</pre>
<p><b>Example 2:</b></p>
<pre><b>Input:</b> [3,3,7,7,10,11,11]
<b>Output:</b> 10
</pre>
<p> </p>
<p><b>Note:</b> Your solution should run in O(log n) time and O(1) space.</p>
