<b>Output:</b> 3
</pre>

<b>Output:</b> 1</pre>
<p><strong>Note:</strong><br/>
You may assume that <em>word1</em> <strong>does not equal to</strong> <em>word2</em>, and <em>word1</em> and <em>word2</em> are both in the list.</p>
